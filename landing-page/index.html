Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KanBased to get started.

Why should I use KanBased instead of Trello or any feature rich apps?

I built KanBased because I was frustrated with the bloat and complexity of other kanban apps. For me navigating the app using keyboard shortcuts is a must have. I wanted a very minimal, fast, and open-source kanban board that I could use myself and share with my friends.

I'm not trying to compete with Trello or any other feature rich apps.

If you feel the vibe I'm going for, you can give it a try and see if it works for you üôÇ

Is KanBased really free?

Yes, KanBased is completely free to use. It's open source software with MIT license that you can use for personal and commercial projects without any limitations. Although do note that we cannot support large usage. You can self-host the entire application if you want to support large usage.

How does the instant sync work?

KanBased uses a modern sync engine instead of traditional REST APIs. When you make changes, they're immediately propagated to all connected devices in real-time. No refresh buttons, no loading spinners - just instant updates across all your devices.

Can I use KanBased offline?

While it's possible to read your boards / notes offline, it's not yet enabled at the moment

How many team members can I invite?

There are no limits on team size. But we recommend keeping it small since it's open source and free to use. You can self-host the entire application if you want to support large teams.

How do I get support?
